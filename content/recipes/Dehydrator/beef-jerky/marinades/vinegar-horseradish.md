Ingredients:
- Prepared horseradish (3 tbsp)
- White vinegar (1/3 cup)
- Salt (1/2 tsp)
- Garlic powder (1/2 tsp)
- Black pepper (1 tsp)

Notes:
- If you can't find prepared horseradish, you can substitute 4.5 tbsp of horseradish sauce.
